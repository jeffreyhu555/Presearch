What is Presearch?

Presearch is a advanced bookmarking google extension. Presearch allows you to view the url and title of a link by hovering over it. You can also add the link to files that can later be accessed without having to click on the link. In the future we expect to increase the functionality of this extension by also providing a basic of the page and a word cloud generated by scraping the page's text.

How to get Presearch

First download presearch from this website. After successfully dowloading and unzipping the .zip file, go to chrome and, in the adress bar, type "chrome://extensions". Click on the button that says "Load unpacked extension" and choose the master-presearch file. Make sure that your new presearch extension is enabled and you're good to go.
